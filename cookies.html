best chocolate chip cookie recipe ever in the history of the world !

follow this one rule to make the best chocolate chip cookies every damn time: STICK TO THE RECIPE

ingredients:

  • 1 cup salted butter softened
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 cup light brown sugar packed
  • 2 tsp pure vanilla extract
  • 2 largge egs room temp
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp sea salt
  • 2 cups chocolate chips

instructions

  1. preheat oven to 375F
  2. line a baking pan with parchment paper and set aside
  3. in a separate bowl mix flour, baking soda, salt, baking powder. set aside
  4. cream together butter and sugars until combined
  5. beat in eggs and vanilla until fluffy
  6. mix in the dry ingredients until combined
  7. add 12oz package of chocolate chips and mix well
  8. roll 2-3 tbs of dough at a time into balls and place evenly spaced on your prepared baking pan
  9. bake in preheated oven for approx 8-10 minutes
  10. take them out when BARELY starting to turn brown
  11. let them sit on the baking pan for 2 minutes before removing to cooling rack

notes

  • when you remove the cookies from the oven they will still look doughy. THIS is the secret.
  • please, for the love of chocolate chip cookies, DO NOT OVERBAKE!
